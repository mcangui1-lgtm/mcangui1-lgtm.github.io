Cirrus Clouds

Cirrus clouds are whispy, thin cloulds that are formed by ice crystals. They are a sign of fair, neutral weather.

Cumulus Clouds

Cumulus clouds are fluffy clouds that can produce rain if they grow too long.

Nimbus Clouds

Nimbus clouds are basically a combination of all other cloud types, being the largest and the type to produce rain and heavy storms.

Stratus Clouds

Stratus clouds widespread and form a blanket-like structure along the sky. They genreally appear the north of warm fronts.
